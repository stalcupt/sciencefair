Calling all scientists!

The theme of this year's fair is "The Earth", and we invite all students up to 8th grade to participate. For those who may want to compete with the chance to move on to the regional fair, the deadline to complete projects is February 11, 2023 for virtual judging that week. For all others, the tentative deadline for completion will be May 8, 2021. Fill out the registration form to sign up.

Due to uncertainties surrounding the pandemic, the date, format, and location (virtual/indoors/outdoors) of the main fair in May has not been confirmed. The PTO will announce the fair date and location in the coming weeks or months. At a minimum, a public fair will be held virtually.

Please note: your project does not have to be about The Earth. Your project should be about whatever you are excited about!

How to make a great science fair project

  • Find any topic that interests you
  • Ask a question
  • Make a prediction
  • Do an experiment to answer your question
  • Write down the results
  • Make a poster to share your project

Remember: plan a safe experiment, free from extreme hazards, explosives, and infectious agents.
